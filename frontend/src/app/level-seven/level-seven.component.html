Did you know that when you have a many-to-many relationship between tables, the connection between those two tables is established by a junction table?
This junction table includes the primary keys of both tables as foreign keys and may also store additional information specific to the relationship between the two tables.

One lecture can have multiple restrictions, and a single restriction can be linked to multiple lectures. This means that there is a many-to-many relationship between the two tables, "Lecture" and "Restriction".
The connection between those two tables is established by a junction table called "LectureToRestriction".
